Purpose

We are an organization that promotes local entrepreneurship in Wake Forest NC. We love where we live and we see the opportunity the area provides. Our goal is to encourage people to start/grow their business in Wake Forest by building and starting relationships with like minded people who share our vision.

We also like beer.
